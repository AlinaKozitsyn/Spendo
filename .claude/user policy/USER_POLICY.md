## 1. Overview & Acceptance

### 1.1 What is Spendo?

**Spendo** is a personal finance analytics platform that helps you understand, control, and plan your spending. You upload credit card or bank statements (in Excel or CSV format), and Spendo:

- **Categorizes transactions** automatically (e.g., "Groceries," "Dining," "Entertainment")
- **Visualizes spending patterns** through interactive dashboards
- **Tracks budgets** in real-time
- **Provides insights** about your spending habits

### 1.2 Key Principles

**What we do:**
- ✓ Help you understand where your money goes
- ✓ Protect your financial data with industry-leading security
- ✓ Give you complete control over your data
- ✓ Never sell your data to third parties
- ✓ Comply with all applicable laws (GDPR, local regulations)

**What we don't do:**
- ✗ Store your credit card numbers, CVV codes, or passwords
- ✗ Give you financial advice (we're a tool, not an advisor)
- ✗ Share your data with advertisers or marketers
- ✗ Process payments on your behalf
- ✗ Guarantee any financial outcomes

### 1.3 Acceptance of Terms

**By using Spendo, you agree to:**
- This User Policy
- Our Privacy Policy
- Our Terms of Service
- Our Acceptable Use Policy

If you don't agree, **do not use Spendo**. If you have questions, contact us at **support@spendo.com** before signing up.

## 2. User Rights & Responsibilities

**What we will NEVER do:**

### 3.3 Data Sharing

**We only share your data with:**

1. **Service Providers** (to run Spendo)
   - Cloud provider (AWS) — your data encrypted at rest
   - Email service (SendGrid) — only sends emails, doesn't see transaction data
   - Payment processor (if you pay for premium) — only transaction ID, not data

2. **Legal Compliance**

3. **AI Classification** (Claude API)
   - **Only**: Merchant name + amount
   - **Never**: User ID, dates, personal info
   - **Encrypted**: Data encrypted end-to-end
   - **Details**: See Section 3.4

### 3.4 Claude API & AI Classification

**How it works:**
1. You upload a statement with transactions
2. Spendo sends to Claude API: `{"merchant": "SQ *COFFEE SHOP", "amount": 45.50}`
3. Claude responds: `{"category": "Dining", "confidence": 0.95}`
4. Spendo categorizes the transaction locally

**What Claude API sees:**
- ✓ Merchant names (e.g., "Rami Levy Supermarket")
- ✓ Transaction amounts (e.g., 123.45)
- ✗ Your name, email, user ID
- ✗ Transaction dates
- ✗ Other personal information

**Your control:**
```
Account Settings → Privacy → Claude API Usage
☐ Allow Claude API for categorization (checked by default)
   [Provides better accuracy with 2-3% improvement]
☐ Opt-in to anonymized research data sharing
   [Helps Spendo improve, no personal data shared]
```

**Anthropic's privacy policy:** https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

### 5.1 What Data Spendo DOES NOT Handle

**Important: Spendo is NOT a bank or payment processor.**

We do NOT:
- ✗ Store credit card numbers
- ✗ Process payments
- ✗ Hold money on your behalf
- ✗ Provide banking services
- ✗ Guarantee account security (that's your bank's job)
- ✗ Offer FDIC insurance

**If your bank account is compromised, contact your bank, not Spendo.**

### 5.2 What Data Spendo DOES Handle

We safely store:
- Merchant names (where you spent money)
- Amounts (how much you spent)
- Dates (when you spent)
- Categories (what you spent on)
- Your budget limits
- Your financial goals

**All encrypted at rest** with AES-256 encryption.

### 5.3 Liability Disclaimer

**Spendo is a TOOL, not a financial advisor.**

- We don't provide investment advice
- We don't predict market performance
- We don't guarantee budget accuracy (depends on your input)
- We don't promise to catch fraud (your bank does)
- Our insights are informational only

**Example: If Spendo says "Spending up 20%" but you overspend anyway, that's not our liability. We're showing you the data; you make the decisions.**

### 5.4 Security Responsibility

**You're responsible for:**
- Keeping your password secure
- Enabling 2FA/MFA
- Logging out on shared devices
- Protecting your device (malware, viruses)
- Verifying your bank statements are accurate before uploading

**We're responsible for:**
- Encrypting data at rest
